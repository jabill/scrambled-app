How to Play

1

Choose Settings

Select your word length (3-7 letters) and timer duration (10-30 seconds)

2

Tap Letters

Tap the scrambled letters to place them in your answer. Rearrange until you've formed a word!

3

Submit Answer

Press Submit when ready. Correct letters in the right position turn green - use that feedback!

4

Beat the Clock

You get 2-3 tries depending on word length. Solve before time runs out to win!

🗓️ Daily Challenge

Every day at midnight (your local time), a new challenge appears - a 6-letter expert word with a 30-second timer and 3 tries. The same word challenges players worldwide. Complete it to build your streak, but remember: you only get one attempt per day. Don't break the chain!

Frequently Asked Questions

How many tries do I get?
You get 2 tries for 3-5 letter words, and 3 tries for 6-7 letter words. After each try, correctly placed letters turn green to help you!
How does scoring work?
Base score equals word length. Timer bonus ranges from 3× (10 seconds) to 1× (30 seconds). Example: a 5-letter word with 10s timer = 5 × 3 = 15 points!
What's the difference between Normal and Expert mode?
Normal mode uses common everyday vocabulary, while Expert mode includes more challenging words. Expert mode is unlocked by long-pressing the 6 or 7 word length button!
Can I play without a timer?
Yes! Choose Casual Mode for a relaxing, no-pressure experience. Perfect for learning new words at your own pace.
When does the Daily Challenge reset?
The Daily Challenge resets at midnight in your local timezone. You get one attempt per day to solve it and build your streak!
Where do the words come from?
All words are sourced from trusted dictionaries and carefully curated to be appropriate, clear, and fair. We filter by real-world usage frequency so Normal mode feels natural while Expert mode provides a genuine challenge.
Does the app collect my data?
No! All your game data stays on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or share any personal information. See our full privacy policy below.
Are there ads or in-app purchases?
Nope! Scrambled is completely free with no ads and no in-app purchases. Just pure word puzzle fun!
Can I play offline?
Yes! Once the app is installed, you can play anywhere, anytime - no internet connection required.
