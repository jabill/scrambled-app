Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Scrambled?

Scrambled is simple to learn:

  • Choose your word length and timer setting, press Play
  • Tap the scrambled letters to place them in your answer
  • Press Submit when ready
  • Correctly placed letters turn green to guide you
  • You get 2-3 tries depending on word length
How does the Daily Challenge work?

Every day at midnight (your local time), a new 6-letter expert word appears. All players worldwide get the same word. You have one attempt to solve it. Complete daily challenges to build your streak!

How do I unlock Expert mode?

Go to Settings, toggle on Expert Mode.

Can I play without a timer?

Yes! Choose "Casual Mode" (the coffee cup icon) for a relaxed, no-timer experience. Perfect for learning new words at your own pace.

How is my score calculated?

Score = word length × timer multiplier. Shorter timers give higher multipliers:

  • 10 seconds = 3× multiplier
  • 20 seconds = 2× multiplier
  • 30 seconds = 1× multiplier

Example: A 5-letter word with 10s timer = 5 × 3 = 15 points

Does Scrambled collect my data?

No! All your game data stays on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or share any personal information. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I clear my statistics?

Go to Stats (bar graph icon) → Scroll down → Tap "Reset All". This will reset all your game statistics while keeping your preferences. [Note: It will also remove the bonus 7-letter level if you've found it!]

Can I play offline?

Yes! Scrambled works completely offline. All 13,450+ words are stored in the app. No internet connection required.

Troubleshooting

App won't open or crashes on startup

Try these steps:

  • Force close the app completely
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure you have the latest version from your app store
  • If issues persist, try uninstalling and reinstalling

Daily Challenge not resetting

Daily challenges reset at midnight in your local timezone. If you've changed time zones or adjusted your device clock, the reset time may be affected. The challenge should reset properly at the next midnight.

Lost my stats after update

All stats are stored locally on your device. If you:

  • Uninstalled the app - stats are permanently deleted
  • Cleared app data - stats are reset
  • Got a new device - stats don't transfer automatically

We recommend not clearing app data to preserve your statistics.

Game feels too easy/hard

Adjust the difficulty by:

  • Changing word length (seek the hidden 7-letter option)
  • Adjusting timer duration (10-30 seconds)
  • Trying Expert mode for harder words (Settings)
  • Using Casual mode for practice without time pressure
